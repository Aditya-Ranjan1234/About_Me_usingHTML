The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

A heartwarming comedy-drama that challenges traditional notions of success and education, delivering a powerful message about following one's passion and pursuing happiness.

The Dark Knight

A gripping and gritty superhero epic that transcends its genre, featuring a mesmerizing performance by Heath Ledger as the iconic villain, the Joker.

The Shawshank Redemption

A deeply moving tale of hope, friendship, and redemption, demonstrating the power of the human spirit to triumph over adversity.
