My Entertainment List

My Top 5 Movies of All Time

1. 3 Idiots

A heartwarming comedy-drama that challenges traditional notions of success and education, delivering a powerful message about following one's passion and pursuing happiness.

2. Extraction

An intense action-thriller following a black ops mercenary on a deadly mission in Dhaka, featuring breathtaking stunts and compelling storytelling.

3. Kingsman: The Secret Service

A stylish and action-packed spy film that combines humor, wit, and adrenaline-pumping action, offering a fresh take on the genre.

4. Deadpool

A hilarious and irreverent superhero film featuring Ryan Reynolds as the wise-cracking mercenary, Deadpool, known for its humor, action, and breaking the fourth wall.

5. Train to Busan

An intense and emotional zombie apocalypse thriller set on a train bound for Busan, showcasing themes of sacrifice, survival, and humanity.

My Top 5 TV Series of All Time

1. Money Heist

A gripping Spanish heist crime drama that follows a group of robbers as they plan and execute elaborate heists, blending suspense, drama, and intricate storytelling.

2. Lucifer

A witty and captivating series following Lucifer Morningstar, the Devil, as he abandons his throne in Hell to run a nightclub in Los Angeles and assist the LAPD in solving crimes.

3. Breaking Bad

A groundbreaking drama that chronicles the transformation of a high school chemistry teacher, Walter White, into a ruthless drug kingpin, exploring themes of morality, power, and consequence.

4. From

A French supernatural thriller series centered around a small town plagued by mysterious and deadly phenomena, weaving together elements of horror, suspense, and drama.

5. Arcane

A visually stunning and emotionally resonant animated series set in the world of League of Legends, exploring the origins and conflicts of iconic characters through rich storytelling and breathtaking animation.
